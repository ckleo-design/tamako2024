Pudding Sandwich Danish

Made with croissant dough rolled with Belgian caramel chocolate, this Danish has fewer layers, which gives the pastry a crisp texture. After baking, it's filled with a smooth pudding made from QP products, similar to creamy custard. The sweetness is well-balanced, making it a great accompaniment to coffee or tea.

Vanilla & Rum Custard

This is a rustic, classic dessert frequently enjoyed in France as an afternoon snack. A crisp tart shell is filled with creamy custard and baked until the top is beautifully caramelised. The custard is made with Okazaki eggs from Atsumi Farms, where hens are fed mugwort, seaweed, and sesame, giving the yolks a rich flavour. The infusion of aromatic vanilla beans and a dash of rum in the custard brings the Flan Parisien layers of fragrance in each bite.

Grand Marnier Strawberry Parfait

The Grand Marnier Strawberry Parfait is a luxurious dessert made using Okazaki eggs from Atsumi Farms, where hens are fed a special diet of mugwort, seaweed, and sesame. This parfait layers velvety egg and vanilla pudding with delicate ladyfinger sponge and a Grand Marnier Sabayon, topped with fresh strawberries and strawberry Grand Marnier gelee for a fruity finish.

Tsukimi Moon Bun

This soft soufflé sponge pays homage to the Autumn moon viewing in Japan. It is filled with a luscious yet delicate creme patissiere made using premium Japanese Mangetsu Tamago eggs, known for their bright orange yolks that resemble a full moon. Mangetsu Tamago is rich in Omega 3 fatty acids, allowing you to indulge guilt-free in this sweet treat.

Moon Creme

The signature creme caramel is made with Mangetsu Tamago to achieve a rich texture bursting with flavour. This premium Japanese egg takes the classic pudding to new heights with its creamy, smooth consistency that perfectly complements the caramel's sweetness. It is a simple yet stunning dessert that allows the quality of its ingredients to shine.
